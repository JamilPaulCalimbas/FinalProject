About the Owner

This isn't just a business; it's a labor of love dedicated to preserving the sweet taste of Filipino heritage. The heart and soul of every sticky rice delicacy, every tender kakanin, is the enduring story of Merlina and the late Teofilo Calimbas. Their culinary journey, rooted deep in family tradition and a shared passion for authentic Philippine snacks, is the comforting guide that inspires every batch we lovingly prepare.

Decades ago, the Calimbas family started preserving and perfecting the recipes for cherished native delicacies, focusing on the rich textures and flavors of the finest rice and coconut. This isn't just about mixing ingredients; it's a careful act of remembrance. We are committed to the precise, time-honored techniques passed down through generations, ensuring every bite is a respectful, flavorful tribute to our Filipino roots.
